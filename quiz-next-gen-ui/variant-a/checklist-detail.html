Checklist for Offences in Children's Homes

Comprehensive CPS Compliance Review

The 10-Point Checklist requires a systematic approach to the decision to prosecute a child in care, referring to those in local authority care or accommodation, and must be completed before submitting to the CPS.

Whilst current practice dictates that this be completed for all cases going to CPS, best practice would be for this checklist to be completed for all cases involving a child looked after.

NOTE: The 10-Point Checklist is currently being reviewed and will be replaced with a 5-Point Checklist. We will update this to the new 5-point checklist once available.
