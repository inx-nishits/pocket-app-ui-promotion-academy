FEBRUARY 2026

We hope you’ve been enjoying using Pocket Sergeant and listening to the podcast. While this section hasn’t been updated for a little while, a lot has been happening behind the scenes — including a full rebuild of the AI chatbot.

The original chatbot was intentionally released as a test, allowing us to understand how you would use it and whether it genuinely added value. We limited monthly tokens at that stage, but they were quickly used, particularly for suspect interview planning which confirmed just how useful the tool had become.

We’re now preparing to re-launch the chatbot in early March 2026, with a much stronger focus on usability and functionality from day one. This next version is a significant step forward, and while it will launch in a far more refined state, we’ll continue developing and improving it over time. The chatbot is now being treated as a product in its own right, shaped directly by how you use it.
